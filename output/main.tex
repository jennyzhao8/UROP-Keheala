\subsection{Crosstab of TIBU vs.\ Paper Registry Outcomes}

Table~\ref{tbl:crosstab} shows the full cross-tabulation of treatment outcomes
recorded in TIBU against outcomes recorded in the paper registry for all
matched patients. Each cell shows the count and its percentage of the row
total. Colored text indicates the type of discrepancy: {\color{red} red} =
Type~I error: TIBU records success but paper records failure; {\color{green!60!black} green} =
Type~II error: TIBU records failure but paper records success; {\color{blue} blue} =
incomplete data: TIBU has no outcome recorded but paper does.
Bold cells indicate the seven most common TIBU$\to$paper correction pairs.
